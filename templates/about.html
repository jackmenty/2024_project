What do we do here?

We are here to provide all the necessary information whenever you feel like you struggle with your decisions on your trash.
Maybe you have a lot of questions surrounding gardening waste, foods left deep in the fridge for so long,
or even appliances you no longer want to use; we can answer these questions for you!

Why make this website?

There are many official websites that do the exact same thing as ours, like government websites and recycling organisations,
but just never provide enough information about very rare or obscure objects.
That is why we want to provide you with quality information regarding your questions.
